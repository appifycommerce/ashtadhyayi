---
sutra: तत्रजातः
vRtti: अणादयो घादयश्च प्रत्ययाः प्रकृतास्तेषामतः प्रभृत्यर्थाः समर्थविभक्तयश्च निर्दिश्यन्ते ॥ तत्रेति सप्तमीसमर्थाज्जात इत्येतस्मिन्नर्थे यथाविहितं प्रत्ययो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The affixes ordained above or here after, come after a word in the 7th case in construction, in the sense of 'born or grown or originated there or then'.

---
The affixes अण् &c. घ &c. have been taught, but no particular sense was assigned to them. See (4.2.92). This _sutra_ gives one of the senses, and also declares the particular case in which the stem must be, to which the affix is to be added. Thus स्रुघ्नेजातः = स्रुघ्न + अण् = स्रौघ्नः 'born in _Srughna_'. So also माथुरः 'born in _Mathura_', औत्सः 'born in _Utsa_', औदपानः 'born in _Udapana_'. राष्ट्रियः (4.2.93), अवारपारीणः (4.2.93) शाकलिकः (4.2.117), ग्राम्यः (4.2.94), ग्रामीणः (4.2.94), कात्रेयकः (4.2.95) औम्भेयकः (4.2.95).
