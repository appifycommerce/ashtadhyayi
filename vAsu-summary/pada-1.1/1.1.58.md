sutra: न पदान्तद्विर्वचनवरेयलोपस्वरसवर्णानुस्वारदीर्घजश्चर्विधिषु
vRtti: पूर्वेणातिप्रसक्तः स्थानिवद्भाव एतेषु विधिषु प्रतिषिध्यते ॥
vRtti_eng: Not so, in rules relating to the finals of words, to the doubling of letters, to the affixing of _varach_, to the elision of _ya_, to accent, to homogeneous letters, to _anusvara_, to the lengthening of vowels, and to the substitution of _jas_ and _char_ letters.
