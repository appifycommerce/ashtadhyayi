sutra: पूर्वकालैकसर्वजरत्पुराणनवकेवलाः समानाधिकरणेन
vRtti: पूर्वकाल एक सर्व जरत् पुराण नव केवल इत्येते सुबन्ताः समानाधिकरणेन सुपा सह समस्यन्ते, तत्पुरुषश्च समासो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: A case-inflected word denoting an action which naturally precedes in time (_púrvakála_), and the words _eka_' one,' _sarva_ 'all,' _jarat_ 'old,' _purána_ 'ancient,’ _nava- 'new' and _kevala_ 'only,' are compounded with their co-relative case-inflected words which are in the same case with them; and the compound so formed is _Tat-purusha_.
