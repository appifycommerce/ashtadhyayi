sutra: पूर्वापरप्रथमचरमजघन्य समानमध्यमध्यमवीराश्च
vRtti: पूर्व अपर प्रथम चरम जघन्य समान मध्य मध्यम वीर इत्येते सुबन्ताः समानाधिकरणेन सुपा सह समस्यन्ते , तत्परुषश्च समासो भवति  ।।
vRtti_eng: The case-inflected words _purva_ 'prior,' _apara_ 'other,' _prathama_ 'first,' _charama_ 'last,' _jaghanya_ 'hindmost,' _samâna_ 'equal,' _madhya_ 'middle,' _madhyama_ 'middle,' and _vira_. 'hero,' are compounded with words ending with a case-affix and which are in agreement (same case) with them; and the compound is _Tat-purusha_.
