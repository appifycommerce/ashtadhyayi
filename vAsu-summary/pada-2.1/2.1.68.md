sutra: कृत्यतुल्याख्या अजात्या
vRtti: कृत्य-प्रत्ययान्तास्तुल्यपर्यायाश्च सुबन्ता अजातिवचनेन समस्यन्ते, तत्पुरुषश्च समासो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: Words ending with a _kritya_ affix, and the word _tulya_ 'equal,' and its synonyms, are compounded with words which do not denote genus (_játi_) being in the same case with them; and the compound is _Tatpurusha_.
