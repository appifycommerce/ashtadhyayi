---
sutra: यावदवधारणे
vRtti: यावदित्येतदव्ययमवधारणे वर्तमानं सुपा सह समस्यते, अव्ययीभावश्च समासो भवति॥
vRtti_eng: The indeclinable word _yárat_ when it signifies limitation, is invariably compounded with a word ending in a case-affix which is in construction with it, and the compound so formed is called _Avyayibhåva_.
