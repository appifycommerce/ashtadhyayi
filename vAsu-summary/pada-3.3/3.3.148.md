---
sutra: यच्चयत्रयोः
vRtti: यच्चयत्र इत्येतयोरुपपदयोरनवक्लृप्त्यमर्षयोर्गम्यमानयोर्धातोर्लिङ्प्रत्ययो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The affix '_Lin_' comes after a root, the words '_yachcha_' and '_yatra_' being in construction, when the sense is that the action is not believed to be likely, or not to be tolerated.
