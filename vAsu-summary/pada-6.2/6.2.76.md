---
sutra: शिल्पिनि चाकृञः
vRtti: शिल्पिवाचिनि समासेऽणन्त उत्तरपदे पूर्वपदमाद्युदात्तं भवति स चेदण् कृञो न भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: And when such a compound ending in अण् affix denotes the name of a professional man, but not when the second term is कार (derived from कृञ्), the acute is on the first syllable of the first word.
