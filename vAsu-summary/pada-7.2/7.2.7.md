---
sutra: तो हलादेर्लघोः
vRtti: हलादेरङ्गस्य लघोरकारस्य इडादौ सिचि परस्मैपदपरे परतो विभाषा वृद्धिर्न भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: Before an इट् - beginning s-Aorist of the _Parasmaipada_, the short अ of the root gets optionally _Vriddhi_, when the stem begins with a consonant, and the अ is prosodially short by being followed by a simple consonant.
