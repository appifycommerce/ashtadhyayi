---
sutra: फक्फिञोरन्यतरस्याम्
vRtti: फिक्फिञोर्युवप्रत्ययोः प्राग्दीव्यतीयेऽजादौ प्रत्यये विवक्षितेऽन्यतरस्यां लुग् भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: There is optionally _luk_-elision of the _Yuvan_ affixes फक् and फिञ् when a _Pragdivyatiya_ affix beginning with a vowel is to be added.

---
By the last _sutra_, all _Yuvan_ affixes were told to be elided. The present _sutra_ declares an option in the case of the _Yuvan_ affixes फक् and फिञ्. Thus the patronymic of गर्ग is गार्ग्य (IV.1.105); the _Yuvan_ of गार्ग्य is formed by फक् (IV.1.101) गार्ग्यायणः ॥ The word denoting the pupils of _Gargyana_ will be गार्गीय or गार्ग्यायणीयाः; वात्सीयाः or वात्सयायनीयाः ॥ Similarly of the affix फिञ् (IV.1.156). Thus from यस्क, the Patronymic is यास्क (IV.1.112). The _Yuvan_ of the latter is यास्कायनि (IV.1.146). The pupils of _Yaskayani_ will be called यास्कीया or यास्कायनीयाः ॥
