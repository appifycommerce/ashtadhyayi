---
sutra: रक्षोयातूनां हननी
vRtti: निर्देशादेव समर्थविभक्तिः । रक्षः शब्दाद्यातुशब्दाच्च षष्ठीसमर्थाद्धननीत्येतस्मिन्नर्थे यत्प्रत्ययो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The affix यत् comes in the _Chhandas_ in the sense of 'killer', after the words '_raksha_' and '_yatu_' in the sixth case in construction.
---
That by which anything is killed is called हननी ॥ Thus रक्षस्य॑ and यातव्य॑ meaning 'that which kills the demons called _Rakshas_ and _Yatus_'. As याते अग्ने रक्षस्या तनः i. e. रक्षसां हननी 'O _Agni_ thy bodies are killers of _Rakshas_'. So यातव्याः तनूः ॥ The word is in the plural as a mark of respect.
