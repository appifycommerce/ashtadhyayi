---
sutra: परिमुखं च
vRtti: परिमुखशब्दाद् द्वितीयासमर्थाद्वर्ततइत्येतस्मिन्नर्थे ठक् प्रत्ययो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The affix ठक् comes in the sense of \"it exists,\" after the word \"_parimukha_,\" being in the second case in construction.
---
Thus परिमुखं वर्तते = पारिमुखिकः 'being before the face,' 'being near or present'.

The word च in the _sutra_ implies that the affix applies to other words also not mentioned. As पारिपार्श्विकः ॥ The word परिमुख is an _Avyayibhava_ compound (II. I. 12). If परि has the force of exclusion (I. 4. 88), then पारिमुखिकः will mean 'a servant who always avoids the face of his master' "स्वामिनो मुखं वर्जयित्वा सेवको वर्त्तते," and if परि means 'all round,' then the word will mean "a servant who is always in the presence of his master -- यतः स्वामिनो मुखं ततस्ततो वर्त्तते"॥
