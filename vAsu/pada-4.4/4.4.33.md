---
sutra: रक्षति
vRtti: तदिति द्वितीयासमर्थाद्रक्षतीत्येतस्मिन्नर्थे ठक् प्रत्ययो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The affix ठक् comes after a word in the second case in construction, when the sense is \"he aids or protects that\".
---
Thus समाजं रक्षति = सामाजिकः 'a spectator', lit. 'who aids an assembly by his presence'. So also सांनिवेशिकः ॥
