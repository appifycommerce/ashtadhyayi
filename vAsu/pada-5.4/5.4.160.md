---
sutra: निष्प्रवाणिश्च
vRtti: निष्प्रवाणिरिति नदीलक्षणस्य कपः प्रतिषेधो निपात्यते ॥
vRtti_eng: "The word निष्प्रवाणि is irregularly formed, without adding the affix कप्, though the last word is a _Nadi_ word."
---
The word प्रवाणी means either प्रीयतेऽस्यां or प्रवयन्ति तया ॥ Here ल्युट् is added to the root वि 'to weave': and denotes the instrument of weaving i. e. 'a shuttle'. निर्गता प्रवाणी अस्य = निष्प्रवाणिः पटः, निष्प्रवाणिः कम्बलः = अपनीतशलाकः "a new unbleached cloth or blanket", from which the shuttle has not yet been extracted, fresh from the loom. The न is changed to ण by (VIII. 4. 29).
