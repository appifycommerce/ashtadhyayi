---
sutra: एकस्य सकृच्च
vRtti: एकशब्दस्य सकृदित्ययमादेशो भवति सुच्च प्रत्ययः क्रियागणने कृत्वसुचोऽपवादः ॥
vRtti_eng: The word सकृत् is substituted for एक before the affix सुच्, when an action is to be counted.

---
This debars कृत्वसुच् ॥ The 'repetition' being impossible, the action alone is counted : so the _anuvritti_ of क्रिया only runs into this _sutra_ and not of अभ्यावृत्तिः ॥ Thus सकृद्भुङ्क्ते "he eats once" सकृदधीते ॥ The स् of सुच् is elided by (8.2.23). But not here in एकः पाकः as being against usage.
