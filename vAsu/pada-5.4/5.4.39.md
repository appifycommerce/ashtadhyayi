---
sutra: मृदस्तिकन्
vRtti: मृच्छब्दात्स्वार्थे तिकन् प्रत्ययो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The affix तिकन् comes after मृद् (the word retaining its sense).

---
Thus मृत्तिका = मृद् 'mud'. Of course this is also an optional affix not a _nitya_ affix like many of the _svarthika_ affixes enumerated in (5.4.7).
