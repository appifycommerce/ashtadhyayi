---
sutra: प्रतियोगे पञ्चम्यास्तसिः
vRtti: प्रतिना कर्म्मप्रवचनीयेन योगे या पञ्चमी विहिता तदन्तातसिः प्रत्ययो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The affix तसि comes after that Ablative case which is ordained owing to the union with the _Karma_-_pravachaniya_ word प्रति ॥ 
---
This Ablative case is ordained by _Sutra_ (II.3.11). Thus प्रद्युम्नः वासुदेवतः प्रति, अभिमन्युरर्जुनतः प्रति ॥ The _anuvritti_ of वा "optionally" runs here also: thus it is an optional rule; we have the regular forms वासुदेवात् &c, also.

Vart:- The affix तसि comes after the words आदि &c: as, आदितः, मध्यतः, पार्श्वतः, पृष्ठतः &c. This is an _akritigana_, no complete list being given.
