---
sutra: षष्ठ्या व्याश्रये
vRtti: नानापक्षसमाश्रयो व्याश्रयः । व्याश्रये गम्यमाने षष्ठ्यन्ताद् वा तसिः प्रत्ययो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The affix _tasi_ comes optionally after a word ending in the sixth case, when the sense is that of a party-name or faction.
---
Taking sides with one faction or another is called व्याश्रयः ॥ Thus देवा अर्जुनस्य पक्षेऽभवन् = देवा अर्जुनतोऽभवन् ॥ 'The _Devas_ became _Arjunites_'. So also आदित्याः कर्णतोऽभवन्. "The _Adityas_ became _Karnaites_".

Why do we say "when it denotes a Party-name"? Observe, वृक्षस्य शाखा "the branch of the tree".
