---
sutra: बृहत्या आच्छादने
vRtti: कन्ननुवर्त्तते, न प्रतिषेधः । बृहतीशब्दादाच्छादने वर्त्तमानात्स्वार्थे कन्प्रत्ययो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The affix _kan_ comes after the word _brihati_ when it means 'an article of dress'.

---
The word कन् is to be read into this _sutra_ and not its prohibition. The force of this कन् is nil, or it is an example of स्वार्थे कन् ॥ Thus बृहती + कन् = बृहतिका (प्रावारः) (7.4.13) (by which long vowel is shortened) "an upper garment or mantle".

Why do we say "when it means dress"? Observe बृहती छन्दः "the metre called _Brihati_".
