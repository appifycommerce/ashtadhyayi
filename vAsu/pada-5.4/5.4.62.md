---
sutra: निष्कुलान्निष्कोषणे
vRtti: कृञ इत्येव । निषकुलशब्दान्निषकोषणे वर्त्तमानात्कृञो योग डाच् प्रत्ययो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The affix _dach_ comes after the word _nishkula_ followed by the verb कृ, when the sense is 'to disembowel'.

---
The verb कृ should be read into the _sutra_. The word निष्कोषण means to bring the inner organs out, or to eviscerate. Thus निष्कुल करोति पशून् "he disembowels or guts the animals". निष्कुला करोति दाडिमम् "he takes out the kernel or seeds of the pomegranite". Why do we say "when meaning to disembowel or take out the kernel"? Observe निष्कुलान् करोति शत्रून् "he makes the enemies family-less".
