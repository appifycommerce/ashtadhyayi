---
sutra: अग्राख्यायामुरसः
vRtti: उरः शब्दान्तात्तत्पुरुषाट्टच्प्रत्ययो भवति स चेदुरः शब्दोऽग्राख्यायां भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The affix _tach_ comes after the word _uras_ standing at the end of a _Tatpurusha_ compound, when it has the sense of 'the most excellent of its kind'.
---
The word अग्र means 'the principal': as the word उरस् is the principal member of an animal, so by metonomy it has come to mean 'the principal'. Thus अश्वानां उरः = अश्वोरसम् so also हस्त्युरसम् "the most excellent of horses or elephants".

Why do we say "when meaning the most excellent". Observe देवदत्तस्योरः = देवदत्तोरः "the breast of _Devadatta_".
