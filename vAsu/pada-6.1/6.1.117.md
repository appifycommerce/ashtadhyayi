---
sutra: यजुष्युरः
vRtti: उरः शब्द एदन्तो यजुषि विषयेऽति प्रकृत्या भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "In the _Yajur_-_Veda_, the word उरस् when changed to उरो, retains its original form when followed by a short अ which is also retained."
---
Thus उरो अन्तरिक्षम् (_Yaj_. _Veda_ _Vajasan_. IV. 7). Some read the _sutra_ as यजुष्युरोः ॥ They take the word as उरू ending in उ, which in the Vocative case assumes the form उरो ॥ They give the following example उरो अन्तरिक्षं सजूर् ॥ But in the _Yajur_ _Veda_ VI. II, the text reads उरोरन्तरिक्षम् सजूः ॥ In the _Yajur_ _Veda_, there being no stanzas, the condition of अन्तः पादं does not apply here.
