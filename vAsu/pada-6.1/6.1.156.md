---
sutra: कारस्करो वृक्षः
vRtti: कारस्कर इति सुट् निपात्यते वृक्षश्चेद्भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "The word कारस्कर means 'a tree'."
---  
This word is formed from कार + कृ + ट (III. 2. 21) = कारस्करः ॥ When not meaning a tree, the form is कारकरः ॥ Some do not make this a separate _sutra_, but include it in the next aphorism.
