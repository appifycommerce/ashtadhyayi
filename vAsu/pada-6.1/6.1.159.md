---
sutra: कर्षात्वतो घञोऽन्त उदात्तः
vRtti: कर्षतेर्धातोराकारवतश्च घञन्तस्यान्त उदात्तो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "A stem formed with the _Krita_-affix घञ् has the acute accent on the end-syllable, if it is formed from the root कृष् (कर्षति) or has a long आ in it."
---  
Thus कर्षः, पाकः, त्यागः, रागः, दायः, धायः ॥ This is an exception to (VI. 1. 197) by which affixes having an indicatory ञ् have acute accent on the first syllable. The word कर्ष is used in the aphorism instead of कृष्, to indicate that कृष of _Bhvadi_ _gana_ is affected by this rule, and not कृष् – कृषति of _Tudadigana_. The word कर्षः derived from _Tudadi_ कृष् has acute accent on the first syllable.
