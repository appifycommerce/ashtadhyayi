---
sutra: कितः
vRtti: तद्धितस्येत्येव । तद्धितस्य कितोन्त उदात्तो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: A stem formed by a _Taddhita_ affix having an indicatory क, has acute accent on the end syllable.
---  
Thus ना॒॒डा॒॒य॒॒नः꣡ formed by फक् (IV. 1. 99), so also चा॒॒रा॒॒य॒॒णः꣡ ॥ Similarly आक्षि॒॒कः꣡, शा॒॒लाकि॒॒कः꣡ formed by ठक् (IV. 4. 1).
