---
sutra: अञ्चेश्छन्दस्यसर्वनामस्थानम्
vRtti: अञ्चेः पराऽसर्वनामस्थानविभक्तिरुदात्ता भवति छन्दसि विषये ॥
vRtti_eng: "In the _Chhandas_, the case-endings other than the _sarvanamasthana_, get the acute accent when coming after अञ्चि ॥ "
---  
In the Vedas, a stem ending in the word अञ्च्, has the acute not only on the affixes previously mentioned, but on the accusative plural affix also. Thus इन्द्रा दधीची अस्थभिः (_Rig_ I. 84. 13). Here the word दधीच् had accent on धी by (VI. 1. 222), but by the present _sutra_, the accent falls on the case-affix अस् ॥ Though the _anuvritti_ of "Instrumentals and the rest" was understood here, the word _asarvanamasthana_ has been used here to include the ending शस् also. As प्रतीचा बाहून् प्रतिभङ्व्येषाम् ॥
