---
sutra: ईडवन्दवृशंसदुहां ण्यतः
vRtti: ईड वन्द वृ शंस दुह इत्येतेषां यो ण्यत् तदन्तस्यादिरुदात्तो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The acute accent is on the first syllable of ईड्, वन्द्, वृ, शंस् and दुह्, when they are followed by the affix ण्यत् ॥

---
Thus ई꣡ड्यम्, व꣡न्द्यम्, वा꣡र्यम्, श꣡स्यम्, दो꣡ह्या धेनुः ॥ The two letters ण् and त् being indicatory, the '_nyat_' is not included in '_yat_' of the last _sutra_. The accent would be regulated by त् ॥ The accent of त् however is debarred by this rule. The वृ in the _sutra_ is वृङ् संभक्तौ of _Kriyadi_ class: the वृञ् of स्वादि class takes _kyap_ affix. See (III.1.109).
