---
sutra: समासस्य
vRtti: समासस्यान्त उदात्तो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: A compound word has the acute on the last syllable.
---   
Thus राजपुरुषः ब्राह्मणकम्बलः, कन्याखनः, पटहशब्दः, नदीघोषः, राजदृषत्, ब्राह्मणसमित् ॥ The consonants being held to be non-existent for the purposes of accent, the _udatta_ will fall on the vowel though it may not be final, the final being a consonant. The exceptions to this rule will be mentioned in the next chapter.
