---
sutra: प्यायः पी
vRtti: विभाषेत्येव । ओप्यायी वृद्धावित्यस्य धातोर्निष्ठायां विभाषा पीत्ययमादेशो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: पी is optionally the substitute of the root प्याय् 'to increase' before the _Nishtha_ affixes.

---
The root ओप्यायी वृद्धौ belongs to _Bhvadi_ class. The indicatory आ shows that the affixes त and तवत् are changed into न and नवत् (VIII.2.45). Thus पीनं मुखम्, पीनौ बाहू, पीनसुरः ॥ The option here is also a regulated option (_vyavasthita_-_vibhasha_). The substitution takes place invariably when the root is without _upasarga_, and never when it is preceded by an _upasarga_. Thus प्रप्यानः आप्यानश्चन्द्रगाः ॥ The substitution, however, must take place when the preposition आङ् precedes the root, and the words अन्धुः and ऊधस् are in composition : as आपीनोन्धुः, आपीनमूधः ॥
