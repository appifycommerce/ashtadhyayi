---
sutra: वेञः
vRtti: लिटीत्यनुवर्त्तते । वेञ् तन्तुसंतान इत्यस्य धातोर्लिटि परतः संप्रसारणं न भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "The semivowel of वे 'to weave' is not vocalised in the Perfect."
---
Thus ववौ, ववतुः, ववुः ॥ This root belongs to यजादि class and would have been vocalised before कित् affixes by (VI. 1. 15); and before non-कित् affixes the Reduplicate syllable of the Perfect would have been vocalised by (VI. 1. 17). Both vocalisations are prohibited here.
