---
sutra: न व्यो लिटि
vRtti: व्येञ् इत्येतस्य धातोर्लिटि परत आकारादेशो न भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "There is not the substitution of आ for the Diphthong of the root व्ये when the affixes of the Perfect follow."
---
Thus संविव्याय, संविष्ययिथ ॥ The reduplicate of the Perfect is vocalised here by (VI. I. I7). The _Vriddhi_ in संविव्याय takes place by (VII. 2. 115) before the णित् affix णल् ॥
