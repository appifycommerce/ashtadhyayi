---
sutra: अपगुरो णमुलि
vRtti: गुरी उद्यमन इत्यस्य धातोरपपूर्वस्य णमुलि परत एचः स्थाने विभाषा आकार आदेशो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: There is optionally the substitution of आ in the room of the diphthong of the root गुर 'to exert' when preceded by the preposition अप and taking the affix णमुल् ॥

---
The affix णमुल् forming the Absolutive Participle is added by (III.4.22), and the word formed by it is repeated. Thus अपगारमपगारम् or अपगोरमपगोरम् ॥ So also this affix is added by (III.4.53): as, अस्यपगारं युध्यन्ते or अस्यपगोरं युध्यन्ते 'they are fighting with raised swords'.
