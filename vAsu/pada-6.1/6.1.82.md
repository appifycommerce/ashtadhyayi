---
sutra: क्रय्यस्तदर्थे
vRtti: क्रीणातेर्द्धातोस्तदर्थे क्रयार्थे यत्तस्मिन्नभिधेये यति प्रत्यये परतोऽयादेशो निपात्यते ॥
vRtti_eng: In क्रय्य there is substitution of अय् for ए when the sense is that of 'exposed or put out for sale, saleable'.

---
The word क्रय्य is derive from क्री 'to buy', with the affix यत्; the _guna_ ए being changed to य ॥ The word तदर्थ means 'for the purpose of that' i.e., for the purpose of being bought. As क्रय्या गौः, क्रय्यः कम्बलः ॥  Why do we say 'when the sense is that of saleable'? Observe क्रेयं नो धान्यं न चास्ति क्रय्यम् "we want to purchase corn, but it is not put out for sale".
