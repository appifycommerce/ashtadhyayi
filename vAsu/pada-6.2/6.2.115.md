---
sutra: शृङ्गमवस्थायां च
vRtti: शृङ्गशब्द उत्तरपदमवस्थायां संज्ञौपम्ययोश्च बहुव्रीहौ आद्युदात्तं भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: In a _Bahuvrihi_ denoting age, (as well as a Name or a Resemblance), the second member शृङ्ग gets acute on the first syllable.
---  
Thus उद्गतशृङ्गः, द्व्यंगुलशृङ्गः, अंगुलशृङ्गः ॥ Here the word शृङ्ग denotes the particular age of the cattle at which the horns come out, or become one or two inches long. Name :-ऋष्यशृङ्गः comparison : गोशृङ्गः, मेषशृङ्गः ॥ Why do we say when denoting 'age &c'. Observe स्थूलशृङ्गः ॥
