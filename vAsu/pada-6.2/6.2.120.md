---
sutra: वीरवीर्यौच
vRtti: वीर वीर्य इत्येतौ च शब्दौ सोरुत्तरौ बहुव्रीहौ समासे छन्दसि विषये आद्युदात्तौ भवतः ॥
vRtti_eng: "In a _Bahuvrihi_ compound in the _Chhandas_, after सु, the words वीर and वीर्य have acute on the first syllable."
---  
Thus सुवीरेण ते, सुवीरस्ते जनिता (_Rig_ IV. 17. 4) सुवीर्यंस्वश्व्यस् (_Rig_ VIII. 13 36) where सुवीर्य has acute on वी ॥ So also सुवीर्यस्य पतयः स्याम ॥ The word वीर्य is formed by यत् affix and by (VI. I. 213), it would have acute on the first. But its enumeration in this _sutra_ shows that Rule (VI. 1. 213) does not apply to वीर्य ॥ The word वीर्य has _svarita_ on the final in the _Chhandas_, by _Phit_ IV. 9. In the secular literature it is _adyudatta_.
