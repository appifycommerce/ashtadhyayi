---
sutra: आदिश्चिहणादीनाम्
vRtti: कन्थान्ते तत्पुरुषे समासे नपुंसकलिङ्गे चिहणादीनामादिरुदात्तो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "In a Neuter _Tatpurusha_ ending in कन्था, the first syllable of चिहण &c have the acute."
---  
As चि꣡हणकन्थम्, म꣡डरकन्थम्, म꣡डुरकन्थम् ॥ The repetition of the word आदि in this _sutra_, though its _anuvritti_ was present, indicates that the first syllable of the first member gets the acute. The word चिहण is derived from the root चिनोति with क्विप् which gives चित् an हन is formed by adding अच् (III. 1. 134) to हन् ॥ चित् + हन = चिहण the elision of त् is irregular.
