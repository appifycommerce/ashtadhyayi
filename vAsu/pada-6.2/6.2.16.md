---
sutra: प्रीतौ च
vRtti: प्रीतौ गम्यमानायां सुख प्रिय इत्येतयोरुत्तरपदयोस्तत्पुरुषे समासे पूर्वपदं प्रकृतिस्वरं भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The first member of a _Tatpurusha_ preserves its accent, the second term being सुख or प्रिय, in the sense of "agreeable to one, or desired".
---
Thus ब्राह्मण꣡सुखं पायसं "the sweet milk desired by or agreeable to the _Brahmanas_", छात्र꣡प्रियोऽनध्यायः ॥ कन्या॑प्रियो मृदङ्गः ॥ &c. The words ब्राह्मण and छात्र have acute on the final being formed by the affixes अण् and ण (V. 4. 62) respectively (III. I. 3) and कन्या has _svarita_ on the final. When not meaning agreeable to or desired, we have राजसुख꣡म्, राजप्रिय꣡म् ॥
