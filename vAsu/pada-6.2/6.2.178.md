---
sutra: वनं समासे
vRtti: समासमात्रे वनमित्येतदुत्तरपदमुपसर्गात्परमन्तोदात्तं भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: After a preposition, वन has acute on the final in compounds of every kind.

---
Thus प्रवणे꣡ यष्टव्यम्, निर्वणे꣡ प्रणिधीयते, the न changed to ण by (VIII.4.5). The word '_samasa_' is used in the _sutra_ to indicate that all sorts of compounds are meant, otherwise only _Bahuvrihi_ would have been meant.
