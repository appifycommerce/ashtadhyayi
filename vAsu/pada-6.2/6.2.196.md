---
sutra: विभाषोत्पुच्छे
vRtti: उत्पुच्छशब्दे तत्पुरुषे विभाषाऽन्त उदात्तो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: In a _Tatpurusha_, the word उत्पुच्छ may optionally have acute on the final.

---
Thus उत्क्रान्तः पुच्छात् = उत्पुच्छः꣡ or उ꣡त्पुच्छः (6.2.2) ॥ When this word is derived by the affix अच् from पुच्छमुदस्यति = उत्पुच्छयति, then it would always have taken acute on the final by (6.2.144), the present _sutra_ ordains option there also. The rule does not apply to a non-_Tatpurusha_: as, उदस्तं पुच्छमस्य = उत्पुच्छः ॥
