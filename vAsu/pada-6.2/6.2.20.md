---
sutra: वा भुवनम्
vRtti: पतिशब्द उत्तरपदे ऐश्वर्यवाचिनि तत्पुरुषे समासे भुवनशब्दः पूर्वपदं वा प्रकृतिस्वरं भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "The word भुवन may optionally keep its accent in a _Tatpurusha_, before पति denoting 'lord'."
---  
Thus भुवनपतिः꣡ or भु꣡वनपतिः ॥ The word भुवन is formed by क्युन् (_Unadi_. II. 80), and has acute on the first (VI. 1. 197). Though _Sutra_ II. 80 (_Unadi_) ordains क्युन् after भू in the Vedas only, yet on the theory of बहुल (III. I. 3), it comes in the secular literature also, as भुवनपतिरादित्यः ॥
