---
sutra: बह्वन्यतरस्याम्
vRtti: बहुशब्दः पूर्वपदमिगन्तादिषूत्तरपदेषु द्विगौ समासेऽन्यतरस्यां प्रकृतिस्वरं भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: In a _Dvigu_ compound, the word बहु may optionally preserve its accent when followed by an _ik_-ending stem, or by a time-word, or by _kapala_, _bhagala_ and _sarava_.

---
This allows option where the last rule required the accent necessarily. Thus बह्व॑रत्निः or बह्वरत्निः, बहु꣡मास्यः or बहुमास्यः꣡, बहु꣡कपालः or बहुकपालः꣡, बहु꣡भगालः or बहुभगालः꣡, बहु꣡शरावः or बहुशरावः꣡ ॥ The word बहु꣡ has acute on the final being formed by the affix कु (_Unadi_ I. 29). When the उ is changed to व्, as in the first example, the _anudatta_ अ is changed into _svarita_ by _8.2.4): when the first member preserves its accent. In the other alternative, the accent falls on the last syllable.
