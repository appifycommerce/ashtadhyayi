---
sutra: संख्या
vRtti: द्वन्द्वसमासे संख्यावाचि पूर्वपदं प्रकृतिस्वरं भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The Numeral word, standing as the first member of a _Dvandva_ compound, preserves its accent.
---
Thus ए꣡कादश, द्वा꣡दश, त्र꣡योदश or त्रयोद꣡श ॥ The word एक is derived from इण् by कन् affix (_Unadi_ III. 43) and has acute on the first (VI. 1. 197). The त्रयस् is the substitute of  त्रि (VI. 3. 48) and has acute on the final.
