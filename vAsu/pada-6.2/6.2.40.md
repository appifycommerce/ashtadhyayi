---
sutra: उष्ट्रः सादिवाम्योः
vRtti: उष्ट्रशब्दः पूर्वपदं सादिवाम्योरुत्तरपदयोः प्रकृतिस्वरं भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "The word '_ushtra_' retains its accent before '_sadi_' and '_vami_'."
---
Thus उ꣡ष्ट्रसादि and उ꣡ष्ट्रवामि ॥ The word उष्ट्र is derived from उप् by ष्ट्रन् affix (_Unadi_ IV. 162) and has acute on the first (VI. I. 197). This is either a _Karmadharaya_ or a Genitive compound. In some texts the above examples are given with a _visarga_ in the masculine, and not Neuter.
