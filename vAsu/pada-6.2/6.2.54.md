---
sutra: ईषदन्यतरस्याम्
vRtti: ईषदित्येतत्पूर्वपदमन्यतरस्यां प्रकृतिस्वरं भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The word ईषत् when first member of a compound may optionally preserve its original accent.

---
Thus ईष꣡त्कडारः or ईषत्कडारः꣡, ईष꣡त्पिङ्गलः or ईषत्पिङ्गलः꣡ ॥ ईष꣡त् has acute on the final. But in ईषद्भेदः &c, the _Krit_-accent will necessarily take place (VI.2.139); no option being allowed; because the compounds to which the present rule applies are, on the maxim of _pratipadokta_, those formed by ईषत् with non-_Krit_ words under (II.2.7).
