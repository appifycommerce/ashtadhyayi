---
sutra: पापं च शिल्पिनि
vRtti: पापशब्दः शिल्पिवाचिन्युत्तरपदे विभाषाऽऽद्युदात्तो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "The word पाप has optionally acute on the first syllable when followed by a word denoting a professional man."
---
Thus पापनापितः or पापनापितः, पापकुलालः or पापकुलालः ॥ This rule applies to the _pratipadokta_ _samasa_ of पाप in the sense of censure as taught in (II. 1. 54), when it is an appositional compound; and not when it forms a Genitive compound. Thus पापस्यनापितः = पापनापितः ॥
