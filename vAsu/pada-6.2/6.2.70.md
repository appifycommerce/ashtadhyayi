---
sutra: अङ्गानि मैरेये
vRtti: मैरेयशब्द उत्तरपदे तदङ्गवाचीनि पूर्वपदान्याद्युदात्तानि भवन्ति ॥
vRtti_eng: "The first syllable of the word preceding मैरेय, gets the acute, when it denotes the ingredient of the same."
---  
Thus गु꣡डमैरेयः 'the wine _maireya_ prepared from treacle or molasses'. म꣡धुमैरेयः 'the _maireya_ prepared from honey'. Why do we say when denoting 'an ingredient?' Observe परममैरयः꣡ ॥ Why do we say "before मेरेय?" Observe पुष्पासवः꣡ ॥ Every sort of spirituous liquor except सुरा is called मैरेय ॥
