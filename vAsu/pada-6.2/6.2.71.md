---
sutra: भक्ताख्यास्तदर्थेषु
vRtti: भक्तमन्नं तदाख्यास्तद्वाचिनः शब्दास्तदर्थेषूत्तरपदेषु आद्युदात्ता भवन्ति ॥
vRtti_eng: "A word denoting food gets the acute on the first syllable, when standing before a word which denotes a repository suited to contain that."
---
Thus भि꣡क्षावासः, भ꣡क्तकंसः, श्रा꣡णाकंसः, भा꣡जीकंसः ॥ The words like भिक्षा, भक्त &c, are names of food. Why do we say 'when denoting the name of food'? Observe समाशशालयः꣡ (the word समाश = समशनं is the name of an 'action' and not of a 'substance'). Why do we say _tadartheshu_ suited to contain that'? Observe भिक्षा꣡प्रियः, which is a _Bahuvrihi_, and the first member gets acute on the final.
