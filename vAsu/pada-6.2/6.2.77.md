---
sutra: संज्ञायां च
vRtti: संज्ञायां विषयेऽणन्त उत्तरपदेऽकृञः पूर्वपदमाद्युदात्तं भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "Also when such an _upapada_ compound ending in अण् affix denotes a Name, the acute falls on the first syllable : but not when the second term is कार ॥"
---
Thus त꣡न्तुवायः 'a kind of insect, spider'. वा꣡लवायः 'the hill _Valavaya_'. But not so अण् with कृ; as रथकारः꣡ 'the name of a _Brahmana_ caste'.
