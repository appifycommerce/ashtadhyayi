---
sutra: अन्त्यात्पूर्वं बह्वचः
vRtti: ज उत्तरपदे बह्वचः पूर्वपदस्यान्त्यात्पूर्वमुदात्तं भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: In a word consisting of more than two syllables, followed by ज, the acute falls on the syllable before the last.

---
Thus उपस꣡रजः, मन्दु꣡रजः, आमल꣡कीजः, and वड꣡वाजः (though the last two words have _upapadas_ ending in long vowel, the accent is governed by this _sutra_ and not the last). Why do we say "a Polysyllabic first member"? Observe दग्धजानि तृणानि ॥
