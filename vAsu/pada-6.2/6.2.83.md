---
sutra: अन्त्यात्पूर्वं बह्वचः
vRtti: ज उत्तरपदे बह्वचः पूर्वपदस्यान्त्यात्पूर्वमुदात्तं भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "In a word consisting of more than two syllables, followed by ज, the acute falls on the syllable before the last."
---
Thus उपसरजः, मन्दुरजः, आमलकीजः, and वडवाजः (though the last two words have _upapadas_ ending in long vowel, the accent is governed by this _sutra_ and not the last). Why do we say "a Polysyllabic first member"? Observe दग्धजानि तृणानि ॥
