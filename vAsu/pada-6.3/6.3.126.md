---
sutra: छन्दसि च
vRtti: छन्दसि विषयेऽष्टनः उत्तरपदे दीर्घो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: In the _Veda_ also, the long vowel is substituted for the final of अष्टन् before a second member.

---
Thus आग्नेयमष्टाकपालं निर्वपेत्, अष्टाहिरण्या दक्षिणा, अष्टापदी देवता सुमती ॥ The form अष्टापदी is the feminine in ङीप् (IV.1.8) of अष्टपात् (V.4.138) the अ of पाद being elided in a _Bahuvrihi_)), पद् being substituted for पात् by (VI.4.130).

Vart:- The final of अष्टन् is lengthened before गो even in the vernacular, when yoking is meant. Thus अष्टागवं शकटम् ॥
