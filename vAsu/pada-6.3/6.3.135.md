---
sutra: द्व्यचोतस्तिङः
vRtti: द्व्यचस्तिङन्तस्यात ऋग्विषये दीर्घो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: A tense affix ending in अ is lengthened in the _Rig_-_Veda_, when the Verb consists of two syllables.

---
Thus विद्मा हि त्वा सत्पतिं शूर गोनाम् &c. See _Rig_ III.42.6, विद्मा हि तस्य पितरम् &c. But not here देवा भवत वाजिनः, as the verb consists of more than two syllables: not also here आ देवान् वक्षि यक्षि च as the verbs do not end in अ ॥
