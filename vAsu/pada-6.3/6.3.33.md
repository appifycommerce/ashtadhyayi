---
sutra: पितरामातरा च च्छन्दसि
vRtti: पितरामातरा इति छन्दसि निपात्यते ॥
vRtti_eng: In the Vedas the form पितरामातरा is valid.

---
In the ordinary language मातापितरौ is the proper form. The Vedic form is derived by adding अङ् to the first member, and आ is added to the second by (VII.1.39) : and then _Guna_ by (VII.3.110). Thus आ मा गन्तां पितरामातरा च ॥
