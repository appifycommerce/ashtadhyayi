---
sutra: उगितश्च
vRtti: उगितश्च परस्या नद्या घादिषु अन्यतरस्यां ह्रस्वो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The feminine ई (ङी) added to a word formed by a _Taddhita_-affix having an indicatory उ or ऋ, is optionally shortened before the घ &c (VI. 3. 43).
---  
Thus श्रेयसितरा or श्रेयसीतरा, or श्रेयस्तरा, विदुषितरा or विदुषीतरा or विद्वत्तरा ॥ The first is formed by the _Taddhita_ affix ईयसुन्, and the second by क्वसु ॥ In one alternative, there is masculisation also when we get the forms श्रेयस्तरा &c. Or this latter form may be considered to have been evolved from श्रेयस्, to which is added the affixes denoting comparison, and then the feminine affix, in denoting a feminine.
