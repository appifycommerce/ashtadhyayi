---
sutra: उदकस्योदः संज्ञायाम्
vRtti: उदकशब्दस्य संज्ञायां विषये उद इत्ययमादेशो भवति उत्तरपदे परतः ॥
vRtti_eng: उद is substituted for उदक, when the compound is a Name.

---
Thus उदमेघः 'a person called _Udamegha_', उदवाहः "a person named _Udavahah_." The well-known Patronymics from these are औदमेघिः and औदवाहिः ॥ Why do we say 'when it is a Name'? Observe उदकगिरिः ॥

Vart:- उद is the substitute for उदक when it stands as the second member of a compound and denotes a Name: as, लोहितोदः, नीलोदः, क्षीरोदः ॥
