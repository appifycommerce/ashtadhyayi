---
sutra: एकादिश्चैकस्य चादुक्
vRtti: एकादिश्च नञ्प्रकृत्या भवति एकशब्दस्यादुगागमो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The Negative particle remains unchanged in a word which begins with एक, and of this एक, there is the augment अदुक् (अद्).

---
Thus एकेन न विंशतिः = एकान्नविंशतिः, एकान्नत्रिंशत् ॥ These are Instrumental compounds. The augment आदुक् is added to the final of the first member, so that we have optionally two forms एकात् न विंशतिः and एकान् न विंशति by (8.4.45).
