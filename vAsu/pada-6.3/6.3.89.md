---
sutra: दृग्दृशवतुषु
vRtti: दृक् दृश वतु इत्येतेषु परतः समानस्य स इत्ययमादेशो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "स is substituted for समान, before दृक्, दृश and the affix वत् ॥"
---  
Thus सदृक्, सदृशः ॥ The affixes कञ् and क्विन् are added to दृश् under (III. 2. 60). Vart:- which give as the forms दृक् and दृश ॥ Vart:-So also before दृक्षः as सदृक्षः ॥ दृक्ष is formed by क्स affix under (III. 2. 60). _Vartika_. The affix वतु is taken for the sake of the subsequent _sutra_.
