---
sutra: तिरसस्तिर्यलोपे
vRtti: तिरस् इत्येतस्य तिरि इत्ययमादेशो भवत्यञ्चतौ व (अ) प्रत्ययान्त उत्तरपदेऽलोपे । यदाऽस्य लोपो न भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: तिरि is substituted for तिरस् before this अञ्च् when no affix (or व affix) follows, provided that the अ of अञ्च् is not elided.
---  
Thus तिर्यङ्, तिर्यञ्चौ, तिर्यञ्चः ॥ Why do we say 'when there is no elision'? Observe तिरश्चा, तिरश्चे ॥ Here अ is elided by (VI. 4. 138). The word अलोपे in the _sutra_ is ambiguous. It may mean either (1) "where अ is elided" or (2) "Where there is no elision". The latter meaning should be taken however.
