---
sutra: अतो हेः
vRtti: अकारान्तादङ्गादुत्तरस्य हेर्लुग्भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The Imperative affix हि is elided after a stem ending in short अ ॥

---
Thus पच, पठ, गच्छ, धाव ॥ But युहि, रुहि after stems ending in उ. Why do we say "short अ"? Observe लुनीहि, पुनीहि (लुना + हि, the ई substitution by (VI.4.113), should be considered _asiddha_), here हि is not elided after the long आ ॥
