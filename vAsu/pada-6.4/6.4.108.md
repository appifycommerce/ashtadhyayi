---
sutra: नित्यं करोतेः
vRtti: करोतेरुत्तरस्य उकारप्रत्ययस्य वकारमकारादौ प्रत्यये परतो नित्यं लोपो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The elision of उ is invariable before व् and म् in the case of कृ (करोति).

---
Thus कुर्वः and कुर्मः ॥ Thus कृ + उ + व = कुर् + उ + वः (VI.4.110) = कुर् + ० + वः (VI.4.108). Now the _lopa_ being not _sthanivat_, when a vowel is to be lengthened (I.1.58), उ of कु should be lengthened by (VIII.2.77). This contingency is prevented by (VIII.2.79).
