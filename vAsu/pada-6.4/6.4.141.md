---
sutra: मन्त्रेष्वाङ्यादेरात्मनः
vRtti: मन्त्रेषु आङि परत आत्मन आदेर्लोपो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "In the _Mantras_, the beginning of आत्मन् is elided, when the affix of the Instrumental Singular follows."
---
आङ् is the name of the Instrumental singular affix, given by ancient Grammarians. Thus त्मना देवेभ्यः, त्मना सोमेषु ॥ Why in the _Mantras_? Observe आत्मनो कृतम् ॥ Why in the Instrumental Singular? यदात्मन स्तन्नो वरिष्ठा ॥

Vart:- The elision of आ of '_atman_' is found in other cases also, but not so frequently as in the Instrumental, as त्मन्यासमञ्जत मह्यम् ॥
