---
sutra: हलस्तद्धितस्य
vRtti: हल उत्तरस्य तद्धितयकारस्य उपधाया ईति परतो लोपो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "The य् of a _Taddhita_ when preceded by a consonant, and penultimate in a stem, is elided before the feminine ई ॥"
---
The _anuvritti_ of "_taddhita_" ceases, because of its mention in the next _sutra_. Hence this _sutra_ applies to feminine ई only. As गार्गी, वात्सी from गार्ग्य and वात्स्य ॥ Why do we say 'when preceded by a consonant'? Observe कारिकेयी ॥ Why do we say 'of a _Taddhita_'. Observe वैद्यस्य भार्या = वैद्यी ॥
