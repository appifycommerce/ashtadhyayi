---
sutra: क्ति स्कन्दिस्यन्दोः
vRtti: क्त्वाप्रत्यये परतः स्कन्द स्यन्द इत्येतयोर्नकारलोपो न भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: स्कन्द and स्यन्द retain their nasal before the affix क्त ॥

---
Thus स्कन्त्वा, and स्यन्दित्वा or स्यन्त्वा, the इट् being added when स्यन्द is considered as having an indicatory ऊ in the _dhatupatha_. When इट् is added, क्त्वा is no longer कित् by virtue of the rule (I.2.18), and elision would not take place, for no rule of elision would apply in that case.
