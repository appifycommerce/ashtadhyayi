---
sutra: सनः क्तिचि लोपश्चास्यान्यतरस्याम्
vRtti: सनोतेरङ्गस्य क्तिचि प्रत्यये परत आकार आदेशो भवति लोपश्चास्यान्यतास्याम् ॥
vRtti_eng: The long आ is optionally substituted for the final of सन् before the affix क्तिच्; and there is also elision optionally of the Nasal.

---
Thus we have three forms सातिः꣡, सन्तिः꣡ and सतिः꣡ ॥ The word अन्यतरस्याम् 'optionally' has been employed in the _sutra_ for the sake of clearness only; for the विभाषा of (6.4.43) could have been read into it by _anuvritti_. Lest any one should doubt, that the _anuvritti_ of विभाषा had ceased with the last aphorism, this word अन्यतरस्याम् is employed here.
