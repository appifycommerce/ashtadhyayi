---
sutra: णेरनिटि
vRtti: अनिडादावार्द्धधातुके णेर्लोपो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The इ of the verbal stem formed with the affix णि, is elided before an _ardhadhatuka_ affix which does not take the augment इट् ॥
---
This debars इयङ्, the semi-vowel य, the _guna_, _Vriddhi_ and the long substitutions. Thus अततक्षत्, अररक्षत्, आशिशत्, कारणा, हारणा, कारकः, हारकः, कार्यते, हार्यते and ज्ञीप्सति (see (VII. 4. I) for the shortening of the stem of the Aorists in these). Why do we say 'not having the augment इट्?' Observe कारयिता and हारयिता ॥
