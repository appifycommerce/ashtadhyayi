---
sutra: आतो लोप इटि च
vRtti: इडादावार्द्धधातुके क्ङिति चाकारान्तस्याङ्गस्य लोपो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "The final आ of a root is elided before an _ardhadhatuka_ affix with the augment इट् as well as when it begins with a vowel and has an indicatory क् or ङ् ॥"
---
Thus पपिथ and तस्थिथ ॥ Here the affix has the इट् augment. पपतुः, पपुः, तस्थतुः, तस्थुः ॥ Here the affixes are कित् by (I. 2. 5) गोदः, कम्बलदः with the affix क (III. 2. 3). So also प्रदा (fem), प्रधा (fem) by अङ् (III. 3. 106). Before _Sarvadhatuka_ affixes, we have यान्ति, वान्ति, व्यत्यरे and व्यत्यले ॥ The two letter are the Imperfect Ist Person Singular _Atmanepada_ of रा and ला with the affix इ (इट्). When it does not begin with a vowel, we have ग्लायते, दासीय ॥
