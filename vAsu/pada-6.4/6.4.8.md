---
sutra: सर्वनामस्थाने चासंबुद्धौ
vRtti: सर्वनामस्थाने च परतोऽसंबुद्धौ नोपधाया दीर्घो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: In a stem ending in न्, the preceding vowel is lengthened in strong cases, with the exception of the Vocative singular.
---
As राजा, राजानौ, राजानः, राजानम्, राजानौ ॥ So also सामानि तिष्ठन्ति ॥ राजा was राजान्स्, the स् was elided by (VI. I. 68), and म् by (VIII. 2. 7). Why do we say 'in strong cases'? Observe राजनि (Locative Singular); सामनि (Locative Singular). Why do we say 'but not in the Vocative Singular'? Observe हे राजन् ! हे तक्षन् !
