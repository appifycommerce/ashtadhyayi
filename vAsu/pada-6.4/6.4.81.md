---
sutra: इणो यण्
vRtti: इणोङ्गस्य यणादेशो भवति अचि परतः ॥
vRtti_eng: For the इ of the root इण् (एति) is substituted a semivowel (य), before an affix beginning with a vowel.
---
Thus यन्ति, यन्तु, आयन् ॥ This supersedes इयङ् substitution, and is itself superseded by (VII. 2. 115), and (VII. 3. 84) which ordain _Vriddhi_ and _Guna_: on the maxim मध्येऽपवादाः पूर्वान् विधीन् बाधन्ते, नोत्तरात् ॥ "_Apavadas_ that are surrounded by the rules which teach operations that have to be superseded by the _apavada_ operations, supersede only those rules that precede, not those that follow them." So we have अयनम् and आयकः ॥
