---
sutra: छादेर्घेऽद्व्युपसर्गस्य
vRtti: छादेरङ्गस्याद्व्युपसर्गस्य घप्रत्यये परत उपधाया ह्रस्वो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The penultimate of the _Churadi_ छद् is shortened before the affix घ (III. 3. 118), when not preceded by two prepositions (or more).
---
As उरश्छदः, प्रच्छदः, दन्तच्छदः ॥ But समुपच्छादः with two prepositions. The same when preceded by more than two prepositions, as समुपातिच्छादः ॥ The elision of णि of छादि &c., by (VI. 4. 51), should not be considered as _asiddha_ (VI. 4. 22) or _sthanivat_ (I. 1. 57), otherwise there would be no penultimate to be shortened.

Vart:- The prohibition with regard to द्वि should be extended to numbers more than two.
