---
sutra: सर्वनाम्नः स्मै
vRtti: अकारान्तात्सर्वनाम्न उत्तरस्य ङेः स्मै इत्ययमादेशो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "After a Pronominal stem ending in अ, स्मै is substituted for the ए of the Dative."
---
As सर्वस्मै, विश्वस्मै, यस्मै, कस्मै, तस्मै ॥ But भवते where the Pronoun does not end in अ ॥ When अश् is substituted for इदम् (II. 4. 32) in _anvadesa_ sentences, then we have the form अस्मै ॥ But in अत्र इदम् + ङे = अत्र अ + ए = अत्रा + ए, here the preceding word ends in आ and we could not have ए replaced by स्मै ॥ Therefore we infer that स्मै being an _antaranga_ operation should be substituted first, and the _sandhi_ afterward and we get अत्रास्मै ॥ (See (VII. 2. 102)) for the change of यद्, तद् &c into य, त &c). The change of ए into स्मै is _antaranga_ as it depends upon one word, while the एकादेश long आ is _bahiranga_ because it is an operation depending upon two words.
