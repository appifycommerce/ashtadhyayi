---
sutra: नपुंसकाच्च
vRtti: नपुंसकादङ्गादुत्तरस्य औङः शी इत्ययमादेशो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "After a neutral stem, ई is substituted for the nominative and accusative dual औ ॥"
---
Thus कुण्डेः तिष्ठतः, कुण्डे पश्य ॥  कुण्ड + ई = कुण्डे, the अ of कुण्ड required to be elided by (VI. 4. 148). This is, however, not done by the following

Vart:- Prohibition should be stated in the case of the affix शी ॥ Therefore the अ is not elided.

Similarly दधिनी, मधुनी, त्रपुणी, जतुनी ॥ The augment न् is added by (VII. 1. 73).
