---
sutra: क्त्वोयक्
vRtti: क्त्वा इत्येतस्य यगागमो भवति छन्दसि विषये ॥
vRtti_eng: "In the _Veda_, the Absolutive affix क्त्वा gets at the end, the augment य ॥"
---
Thus दत्वाय सविता धियः (for दत्वा), This _sutra_ is not read immediately after (VII. I. 38), as in that _sutra_, the _anuvritti_ of _samasa_ is understood, while there is no such _anuvritti_ here.
