---
sutra: विभाषा चिण्ळमुलोः
vRtti: चिण् णमुल् इत्येतयोर्विभाषा लभेर्नुम्भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The नुम् is optionally added to लभ् not preceded by a Preposition, before the चिण् Aorist and the Absolutive णमुल् ॥

---
Thus अलाभि or अलम्भि, लाभंलाभम् or लम्भंलम्भम् ॥ This is a _Vyavasthita_-_vibhasha_, the option is allowed where there is no Preposition along with the root; and no option is allowed but नुम् must be added, when a Preposition precedes: as प्रालम्भि, प्रलम्भम् ॥
