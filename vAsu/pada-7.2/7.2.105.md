---
sutra: काति
vRtti: अतीत्येतस्यां विभक्तौ परतः किमित्येतस्य क्व इत्ययमादेशो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "क्व is substituted for किम् before the _vibhakti_ अत् (V. 3. 13)."
---
As क्व गमिष्यसि, क्व भोक्ष्यते ॥ The substitute कु of the last rule, before the affix अ would have become क्व, but that it would have caused _guna_, hence this separate substitute. Had the _sutra_ been किमोऽड्वत् it would not have included the अकच् augmented किम् ॥
