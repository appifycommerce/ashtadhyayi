---
sutra: इदमो मः
vRtti: इदमः सौ परतो मकारोन्तादेशो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "म् is substituted for the final म् of इदम् in the Nominative Singular."
---
As इयम्, अयम् ॥ The substitution of म् for म् is to prevent the अ substitution of (VII. 2. 102). The case-ending is elided by (VI. 1. 68), and य substituted by (VII. 2. 110).
