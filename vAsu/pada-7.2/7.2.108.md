---
sutra: इदमो मः
vRtti: इदमः सौ परतो मकारोन्तादेशो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: म् is substituted for the final म् of इदम् in the Nominative Singular.

---
As इयम्, अयम् ॥ The substitution of म् for म् is to prevent the अ substitution of (7.2.102). The case-ending is elided by (6.1.68), and य substituted by (7.2.110).
