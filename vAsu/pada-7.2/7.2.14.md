---
sutra: श्वीदितो निष्ठायाम्
vRtti: श्वयतेरीदितश्च निष्ठायामिडागमो न भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "The Participial affixes त and तवत् (_kta_ and _ktavatu_), do not get the augment इट् after श्वि, and after the root which has an indicatory ई ॥"
---
As शूनः, शूनवान; ओलेजी (VI. I0), - लग्न, लग्नवान्; ओविजी (VI. 9), औद्विग्नः, उद्विग्नवान् ॥ The त is changed to न because of the indicatory ओ (VIII. 2. 45). So also दीपी (IV. 42), दीप्तः, दीप्तवान् ॥ In the _Dhatupatha_, डीङ् (IV. 27), is classed among ओदित् roots, and it indicates that the _Nishtha_ is _anit_ after it : and ओ is for न-change (VIII. 2. 45) as, उड्डीनः, उड्डीनवान् ॥ The word निष्ठायाम् governs the following _sutras_ upto (VII. 2. 35).
