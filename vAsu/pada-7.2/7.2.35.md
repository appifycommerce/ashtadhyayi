---
sutra: आर्द्धधातुकस्येड्वलादेः
vRtti: छन्दसीति निवृत्तम् । आर्द्धधातुकस्य वलादेरिडागमो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: An _ardhadhatuka_ affix (III. 4. 114) &c) beginning with a consonant (except य), gets the augment इट् (in these rules).

---
Thus लविता, लवितुम्, लवितव्यम्, पविता, पवितुम्, पवितव्यम् ॥ Why '_ardhadhatuka_'? Observe आस्ते, वस्ते ॥ The _niyama_ rule of (VII.2.76) ordaining इट् augment to _sarvadhatuka_ affixes of _Rudadi_ verbs, would prevent इट् augment before _sarvadhatuka_ affixes when coming after other roots. The employment of _ardhadhatuka_ here can be dispensed with. Why before affixes beginning with a वल्-consonant? Observe लव्यम्, पव्यम्, लवनीयम्, पवनीयम् ॥ Though the _anuvritti_ of इट् was understood here, its repetition is for the sake of preventing the prohibition of the foregoing _sutras_ like (VII.2.8).
