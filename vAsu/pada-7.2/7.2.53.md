---
sutra: अञ्चेः पूजायाम्
vRtti: अञ्चेः पूजायामर्थे क्त्वानिष्ठयोरिडागमो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The affixes _ktva_, _kta_ and _ktavatu_ take the augment इट् after the root अञ्च्, when meaning 'to honor or do something to show honor'.

---
As अञ्चित्वा जानु जुहोति, अञ्चिता अस्य गुरवः (3.2.188), (6.4.30), (2.3.67) By (7.2.56) अञ्चु would optionally have caused इट् to come before _ktva_, and hence by (7.2.15) never before the _Nishtha_. This _sutra_ ordains invariable addition of this augment. Why do we say when meaning 'to honor'? Observe उदक्तमुदकं कूपात्, 'the water is raised from the well'.
