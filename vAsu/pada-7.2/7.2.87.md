---
sutra: द्वितीयायां च
vRtti: द्वितीयायां च परतो युष्मदस्मदोराकारादेशो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "आ is substituted for the final of युष्मद् and अस्मद् before the endings of the Accusative."
---
As त्वाम्, माम्, युवाम्, आवाम्, युष्मान् and अस्मान् ॥ This rule applies to endings which are even substitutes, otherwise the last rule would have been sufficient. See (VII. 2. 28), (VII. 2. 29), and (VII. 2. 92) and (VII. 2. 97).
