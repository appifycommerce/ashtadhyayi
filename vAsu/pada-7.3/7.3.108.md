---
sutra: ह्रस्वस्य गुणः
vRtti: संबुद्धाविति वर्त्तते । ह्रस्वान्तस्याङ्गस्य गुणो भवति संबुद्धो परतः ॥
vRtti_eng: For short इ and उ final in a nominal-stem, a _guna_ is substituted in the Vocative Singular.

---
As हे अग्ने, हे वायो, हे पटो ॥ But there is no _guna_ in हे कुमारि, हे ब्रह्मबन्धु, because ई and ऊ were shortened specifically, and to substitute _guna_ for them would make their shortening a useless operation. Moreover, had _guna_ been intended in the case of these _nadi_ words, the _sutra_ would have been अम्बार्थानां ह्रस्वः, २ नदीह्रस्वयोर्गुणः ॥
