---
sutra: ऋतो ङिसर्वनामस्थानयोः
vRtti: गुण इति वर्तते ऋकारान्तस्याङ्गस्य ङौ परतः सर्वनामस्थाने च गुणो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: _Guna_ is substituted for the final ऋ of a stem, in the Locative singular and in the Strong cases.

---
As मातरि, पितरि, भ्रातरि, कर्तरि; मातरौ, पितरौ, भ्रातरौ, कर्तारौ, कर्तारः ॥ For the long in कर्तारौ and कर्तारः see (VI.4.11). The त् in ऋत् is for the sake of facility of utterance.

Because before ङि and strong cases, it is impossible for a stem to end in a long vowel ॠ nor a _dhatu_ noun can so come, since in that case, long ॠ would be changed to इर् by (VII. I. 100), and in forms like कर्तॄणि there is नुम् ॥
