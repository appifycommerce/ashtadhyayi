---
sutra: जे प्रोष्ठपदानाम्
vRtti: ज इति जातार्थो निर्दिश्यते । तत्र यस्तद्धितो विहितस्तस्मिन् ञिति णिति किति च परतः प्रोष्ठपदानामुत्तरस्याचामादेरचो वृद्धिर्भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: In प्रोष्ठपद and its synonyms, the first vowel of the second member gets the _Vriddhi_, before a _Taddhita_ affix meaning 'born in that time', and having an indicatory ञ्, ण्, or क् ॥

---
The word ज means 'born', the affixes denoting 'born under that asterism' are meant here. प्रोष्ठपद is the name of an asterism, the time appertaining there to is also called प्रोष्ठपद (the affix अण् of (IV.3.3), is elided by IV.2.4). प्रोष्ठपदासु जातः = प्रोष्ठपादो माणवकः (with अण् (IV.3.16)). Why do we say 'born in that time'? Observe यदा प्रौष्ठपदोमेघः (= प्रोष्ठपदासु भवः) धरणीमभिवर्षति ॥ The plural number प्रोष्ठपदानां indicates that the synonyms of प्रोष्ठपद such as भद्रपाद are also to be included.
