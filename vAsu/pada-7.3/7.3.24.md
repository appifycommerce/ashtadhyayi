---
sutra: प्राचां नगरान्ते
vRtti: प्राचां देशे नगरान्तेङ्गे पूर्वपदस्योत्तरपदस्याचामादेरचो वृद्धिर्भवति तद्धिते ञिति णिति किति च परतः ॥
vRtti_eng: "Before a _Taddhita_ affix having an indicatory or ञ्, ण्, or क्, the _Vriddhi_ is substituted for the first vowels of both members of the compound, which is the name of a city of the Eastern People, and which ends in the word नगर ॥"
---
As सोह्मनागरः (= सुह्मनगरे भवः), पौण्ड्रनागरः ॥ Why do we say 'of the Eastern people'? Observe माद्रनगरः, from मद्रनगरः the city of the Northern people.
