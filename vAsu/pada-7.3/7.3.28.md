---
sutra: प्रवाहणस्य ढे
vRtti: प्रवाहणस्य ढे परत उत्तरपदस्याचामादेरचो वृद्धिर्भवति पूर्वपदस्य वा भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: Before the affix ढ (एय), the _Vriddhi_ is substituted for the first vowel of the second member of प्रवाहण, but optionally so for the first vowel of the first member (i.e. प्र) ॥

---
Thus प्रवाहणस्यापत्यं = प्रावाहणेयः or प्रवाहणेयः ॥ The affix ढक् (4.1.123), is added, similarly प्रवाहणेयी भार्याऽस्य = प्रवाहणेयीभार्यः (6.3.39), masculation prohibited)). Or we may translate the _sutra_ as "Before the affix ढ, the vowel of प्र in प्रवाहण optionally gets _Vriddhi_", and omit the rest. The masculation will still be prohibited by (6.3.41), (जाते)
