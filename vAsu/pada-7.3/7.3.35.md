---
sutra: जनिवध्योश्च
vRtti: जनि वधि इत्येतयोश्चिणि कृति च ञ्णिति यदुक्तं तन्न भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "The _Vriddhi_ is not substituted for the vowels of जन् and वध् before the Aorist-sign चिण् and the _krit_-affixes with an indicatory ञ् and ण् ॥"
---
As अजनि and अवधि with चिण्, and जनकः and वधकः (with ण्वुल्), प्रजनः, वधः ॥ This rule refers to the separate and the distinct root वध and not to the substitute of हन् ॥ This we see in the line भक्षकश्चेन्न विद्यते वधकोऽपि न विद्यते ॥ The form from हन् will be घातकः ॥ Moreover the substitute वध ends with अ i.e. it is of two syllables '_badha_', and as such it also does not admit _Vriddhi_. (See (II. 4. 42)). The prohibition refers to चिण् and _krit_-affixes, therefore not here, as जजान गर्भं महिमानमिन्द्रम् ॥
