---
sutra: भुजन्युव्जौ पाण्युपतापयोः
vRtti: भुज न्युव्ज इत्येतौ शब्दौ निपात्येते यथासंख्यम् पाणावुपतापे च ॥
vRtti_eng: भुज 'an arm', and न्युव्ज 'a kind of bodily disease', are irregularly formed, without any change of their ज before घञ् ॥

---
The word भुजः = भुज्यतेऽनेन, with घञ् (III.3.121). The irregularity consists in the absence of _Guna_, as well as of gutturalisation. न्युव्जिताः शरतेऽस्मिन् = न्युव्जः from the root उव्ज अर्जवे (_Tudadi_ 20). The irregularity consists in the non-changing of ज ॥ When not meaning 'an arm' and 'a disease', we have भोगः, समुद्गः ॥
