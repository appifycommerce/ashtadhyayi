---
sutra: उतो वृद्धिर्लुकि हलि
vRtti: सार्वधातुके पितीति वर्तते ॥ उकारान्तस्याङ्गस्य वृद्धिर्भवति लुकि सति हलादौ पिति सार्वधातुके ॥
vRtti_eng: A root ending in short उ, which has no Present characteristic (i. e. the _vikarana_ is dropped by _luk_ elision), gets _vriddhi_, before a पित् _Sarvadhatuka_ affix beginning with a consonant, but not if the stem is reduplicated.

---
Thus यौति, यौषि, यौमि; नौति, नौषि, नौमि, स्तौति, स्तौषि, स्तौमि ॥ Why ending in उ ? Observe एति, एषि, एमि ॥ Why do we say whose _vikarana_ is elided by लुक्? Observe सुनोति, सुनोषि, सुनोमि ॥  Why beginning with हल् ? Observe यवाणि, रवाणि, the First Person of the Imperative is पित् by (3.4.92). Why before a पित् affix? Observe युतः, रुतः ॥ The augment यासुट् being ङित् (3.4.103), prevents पित् action in अपि स्तुयात् राजानम् ॥ The phrase नाभ्यस्तस्य should be read into the _sutra_ from (7.3.87): therefore _Vriddhi_ does not take place here योयोति तोनोति ॥ Here there is _luk_-elision of यङ् ॥
