---
sutra: ॠदृशोऽङि गुणः
vRtti: ॠवर्णान्तानां दृशेश्च अङि परतो गुणो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "_Guna_ is substituted for the vowel of the root, before the affix अङ्, in the roots ending in ऋ or ॠ, as well as in दृश् ॥"
---
As शकलाङ्गुष्ठकोऽकरत्, अहं तेभ्योऽकरं नमः, असरत्, आरत्, अदर्शत्, अदर्शताम्, अदर्शन् ॥ The affix अङ् means the Aorist-character अङ् of which the above examples are given. It also is the _krit_-affix (III. 3. 104). of which we have जॄ--जरा ॥

The word अकरत् is अङ् Aorist formed from कृ by (III. I. 59). The word असरत् is from सृ (III. I. 56). अदर्शत् by (VI. I. 57).
