---
sutra: ई घ्राध्मोः
vRtti: घ्रा ध्मा इत्येतयोर्यङि परत ईकारादेशो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: Long ई is substituted for the vowel of the roots घ्रा and ध्मा in the Intensive.

---
As जेघ्रीयते, देध्मीयते ॥ The long ई is for the sake of the subsequent _sutra_, short इ would have, by (7.4.25), given the same forms also.
