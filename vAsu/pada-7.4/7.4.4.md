---
sutra: लोपः पिबतेरीच्चाभ्यासस्य
vRtti: पिबतेरङ्गस्य णौ चङ्युपधाया लोपो भवति अभ्यासस्येकारादेशो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "The penultimate vowel, in the Causative stem of पा 'to drink' is elided in the Aorist, and for the vowel of the Reduplicate there is substituted long ई ॥"
---
As पा + णि + अत् = पाय् + इ + अत् (VII. 3. 37) = पाय् + अत् (VI. 4. 51) = प्य् + अत् (VII. 4. 8) = पप्य् + अत् (I. I. 59) = पीप्यत् (VII. 4. 4). Thus अपीप्यत्, अपीप्यताम्, अपीप्यन् ॥ When the penultimate आ is elided, there remains प् which cannot be reduplicated as having no vowel, but the elided आ is considered as _sthanivat_ and thus पा is reduplicated. _Sutra_ (VII. 4. 80) establishes by implication the principle that the substitutes caused by णि are _sthanivat_. Thus भू + णि = भावि; in reduplicating, this भा will not be reduplicated, but भू, as बुभावि, भा = भू by _sthanivad_-_bhava_.
