---
sutra: नकवतेर्यङि
vRtti: कवतेरभ्यासस्य यङि परतश्चुर्न भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The Palatal is not substituted for the Guttural of the reduplicate of कु (कवते) in the Intensive.

---
As कोकूयते उष्ट्रः, कोकूयते खरः ॥ The कु here is कुङ् (_Bhuadi_ 999), and not कु 'to make sound' (_Adadi_ 33, कौति), nor कुङ् (_Tudadi_ 108, कुवति) ॥ Of those two, we have चोकूयते ॥ Why do we say in the Intensive? Observe चुकुवे ॥
