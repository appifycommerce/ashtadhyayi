---
sutra: उरत्
vRtti: ऋवर्णान्तस्याभ्यासस्याकारादेशो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: अर् (I. 1. 51) is substituted for the ऋ or ॠ of the reduplicate.

---
As ववृते, ववृधे, शशृधे, नर्नर्त्ति, नरिनर्त्ति, नरीनर्त्ति ॥ The र् is elided by (VII.4.60). In the Intensives (VII.4.90) &c, are applied, by which after the substitution of अर् by the present _sutra_, we apply those _sutras_ and add रीक रुक् रिक् &c: for the maxim is अभ्यासविकारेषु अपवादो नोत्सर्गान् विधीन् बाधते "so far as the changes of a reduplicative syllable are concerned special (_apavada_) rules do not supersede the general (_utsarga_) rules".
