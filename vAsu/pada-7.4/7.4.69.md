---
sutra: दीर्घ इणः किति
vRtti: इणोङ्गस्य योभ्यासस्तस्य दीर्घो भवति किति लिटि परतः ॥
vRtti_eng: A long vowel is substituted in the reduplicate of the root इ (एति), before a Personal ending of the Perfect, which has an indicatory क् ॥

---
As ईयतुः, ईयुः ॥ These are thus formed इ + अतुस् = य् + अतुः (य् substituted by इ (VI.4.81)). Then there is reduplication, the य् becomes _sthanivat_ to इ by (I. I. 59), and we have इ य् + अतुः and then by the present rule इयतुः ॥ Why do we say before a कित् affix? Observe इयाय, इययिथ ॥
