---
sutra: भृञामित्
vRtti: भृञादीनां त्रयाणामभ्यासस्येकारादेशो भवति श्लौ सति ॥
vRtti_eng: "In the Reduplicated Present-form of the three roots भृ, मा (माङ्) and हा (ओहाङ्), इ is substituted for the vowel of the reduplicate."
---
As बिभर्त्ति, मिमीते, (VI. 4. 113) जिहीते ॥ The word 'three' is understood here, therefore not in ओहाक्-जहाति ॥ The rule applies in the Reduplicated Present system (श्लु) only: therefore not in बभार ॥
