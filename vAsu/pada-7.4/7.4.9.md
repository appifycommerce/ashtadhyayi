---
sutra: दयतेर्दिगि लिटि
vRtti: दयतेरङ्गस्य लिटि परतो दिगीत्ययमादेशो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: दिगि is substituted for दे (दयते), in the Perfect.

---
As अव दिग्ये, अव दिग्याते, अव दिग्यिर ॥ The root देङ् 'to protect' (_Bhuadi_ 1011) is to be taken, and not दय 'to give' (_Bhuadi_ 510), for that root forms its Perfect by आम् (Periphrastic Perfect) as taught in (III. I. 37). The substitute दिगि debars reduplication.
