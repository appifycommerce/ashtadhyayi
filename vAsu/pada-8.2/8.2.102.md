---
sutra: उपरिस्विदासीदिति च
vRtti: अनुदात्तमिति वर्त्तते ॥ उपरिस्विदासीत्येतस्य टेरनुदात्तः प्लुतो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "In उपरि स्विद् आसीद् the end vowel is _anudatta_ and _pluta_."
---
The word _anudatta_ is understood here also. The end-vowel would have been _pluta_ here by (VIII. 2. 97): the present _sutra_ really ordains accent-lessness of this _pluta_.

Thus अधः स्विदासी३त्, उपरि स्विदासी३त् (_Rig_ X. 129. 5). In the first portion आसीत् is _pluta_ and _udatta_ by (VIII. 2. 97), in the second it is _anudatta_ by the present _sutra_.
