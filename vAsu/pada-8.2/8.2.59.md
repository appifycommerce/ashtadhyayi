---
sutra: भित्तं शकलम्
vRtti: भित्तमिति निपात्यते शकलं चेत्तद्भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "The word भित्त is irregularly formed in the sense of 'a fragment, a portion'."
---
Thus भित्तं तिष्ठति, भित्तं प्रपतति ॥ This is synonymous with शकल ॥ The root-meaning of भिद् is not very manifest in this word, it may be taken as a _rudhi_ word. The regular form is भिन्नं under (VIII. 2. 42).
