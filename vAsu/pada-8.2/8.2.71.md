---
sutra: भुवश्च महाव्याहृतेः
vRtti: भुवस् इत्येतस्य महाव्याहृतेश्छन्दसि विषये उभयथा भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: In the _Chhandas_, रु and र may replace the final of the word भुवस् when used as a _maha_-_vyahriti_.

---
Thus भुवरित्यन्तरिक्षम् or भुव इत्यन्तरिक्षम् ॥ The _maha_-_vyahritis_ are three, used generally before the famous _Gayatri_ _mantra_. They denote respectively the earth, the firmament and the heaven. भुवस् is an Indeclinable and a _Vyahriti_ denoting the firmament. The other two are भूः and स्वः ॥

Why do we say when it is a _maha_-_vyahriti_? Observe भुवो विश्वेषु भुवनेषु यज्ञियः ॥ Here भुवः is a verb, 2nd Person Singular, Imperfect (लङ्) of the root भू, without _guna_ of the root, and the अट् augment is not added as a Vedic diversity.
