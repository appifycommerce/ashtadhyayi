---
sutra: ये यज्ञकर्मणि
vRtti: ये इत्येतस्य यज्ञकर्मणि प्लुतो भवति ॥ इष्टिः ॥ ये यजामह इत्यत्रैवायंप्लुत इष्यते ॥
vRtti_eng: "The vowel of ये becomes _pluta_ in a sacred text, when it is employed in a sacrificial work."
---
Thus ये३यजामहे ॥ Why do we say when employed in sacrificial work? Observe ये यजामह इति पञ्चाक्षरम् "_Ye_ _yajamahe_ consists of five syllables". Here it is simple recitation.

_Ishti_:- The word ये is _pluta_ before यजामहे only. Therefore not so here: ये देवा॑सो दि॒व्येकाद॑शस्थ (_Rig_. I. 139. 11).
