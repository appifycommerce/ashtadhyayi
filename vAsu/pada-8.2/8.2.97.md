---
sutra: विचार्यमाणानाम्
vRtti: प्रमाणेन वस्तुपरीक्षणं विचारः । तस्य विषये विचार्यमाणानां वाक्यानां टेः प्लुतो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "The end-syllable of those sentences is _pluta_, which denote acts of reflection (or balancing between two alternatives)."
---
To determine a thing by weighing all arguments pro and con is called _vichara_ or judgment. Thus होतव्यं दीक्षितस्य गृहा ३ इ न होतव्या꣡३म् "should one perform sacrifice in the house of an initiated person". Similarly तिष्ठेद्यूपा३इ, अनुप्रहरेद्यूपा३इ ॥ Here also it is being reflected upon whether यूपे तिष्ठेत् or यूपे अनुप्रहरेत् ॥ i. e. should the stake remain upright or should it be put flat by the _yajamana_. किं यूपस्तिष्ठेत्, किं वा यूपं यजमानः शाययेत् ॥
