---
sutra: पूर्वं तु भाषायाम्
vRtti: भाषायां विषये विचार्यमाणानां पूर्वमेव प्लवते ॥
vRtti_eng: "In the common speech, the end-vowel of the first alternative is only _pluta_."
---
This makes a _niyama_ or restriction. The previous _sutra_, is thus confined to Vedic literature, and not to the vernacular. तु here has the force of 'only'. Thus अहिर् नू꣡३ रज्जुर् नु 'Is it a snake or a rope'? लोष्टो नू꣡३ कपोतोनु 'Is it a clod of earth or a pigeon ?'

The priority, of course, depends upon employment. The order of words depends upon one's choice.
