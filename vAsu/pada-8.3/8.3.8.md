---
sutra: उभयथर्क्षु
vRtti: नकारान्तस्य पदस्य छवि परतः अम्परे उभयथा ऋक्षु भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "In both ways, in the _Rig_ verses."
---
This ordains an option to the last _sutra_, by which the रु substitution was compulsory. A word ending in म् followed by a letter of छव् class, which itself is followed by अम्, changes its final म् to र् optionally in the _Rig_ _Veda_. Sometimes there is र् and sometimes म् ॥ Thus तस्मिन् त्वा दधाति or तस्मिँस् त्वा दधाति; पशूँस्ताँश्चक्रे ॥

Why do we say "in the _Rig_ verses". No option is allowed here ताँस्त्व खाद सुखादिताम् ॥
