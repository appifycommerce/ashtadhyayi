---
sutra: अयनं च
vRtti: अन्तरदेश इति वर्त्तते । अयननकारस्य चान्तः शब्दादुत्तरस्य णकारादेशो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The न of अयन is changed to ण, when preceded by the word अन्तर्, and the word does not denote a country.

---
Thus अन्तरयणं शोभनम् ॥ Why do we say "not denoting a country"? Observe अन्तरयनो देशः ॥ This ण change would have taken place by (VIII.4.29), this special _sutra_ is for आदेश purposes. The word अयन is formed by ल्युट् added to अय् or इण् ॥
