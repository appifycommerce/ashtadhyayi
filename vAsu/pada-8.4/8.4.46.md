---
sutra: अचो रहाभ्यां द्वे
vRtti: अच उत्तरौ यौ रेफहकारौ ताभ्यामुत्तरस्य यरो द्वे भवतः ॥
vRtti_eng: "There is reduplication of यर्, i. e. all the consonants except ह, after the letters र् and ह् following a vowel."
---
The word यर् of last _sutra_ is understood here. According to others, the वा is also understood, and this is an optional rule. Thus अर्क्कः, मर्क्कः, ब्रह्म्मा, अपह्न्नुते ॥

Why do we say अचः 'following a vowel'? Observe ह्नुते, ह्मलयति ॥
