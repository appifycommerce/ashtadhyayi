---
sutra: शरोचि
vRtti: शरोचि परतो न द्वे भवतः ॥
vRtti_eng: There are not two in the room of a sibilant (शर्), when a vowel follows.

---
The word न is to be read into the _sutra_. This debars the application of rule 46 _ante_. Thus कर्षति, वर्षति, आकर्षः, अक्षदर्शः ॥

Why do we say अचि 'when a vowel follows'? Observe दर्श्श्यते ॥
