---
sutra: पानं देशे
vRtti: पाननकारस्य पूर्वपदस्थान्निमित्तादुत्तरस्य देशाभिधाने णकार आदेशो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "The न of पान is changed into ण, when it occurs as the second member of a compound, the first member of which contains a letter causing change; and the whole compound denotes a country or a people."
---
The word पान is formed by the affix ल्युट् (III. 3. 113). Thus क्षीरपाणा उशीनराः = क्षीरपाणं येषां 'the milk-drinking _Usinaras_'. सुरापाणाः प्राच्याः 'the wine-drinking _Prachyas_'. सौवीरपाणा बाह्लीकाः "the _sauvira_-drinking _Bahlikas_". कषायपाणा गान्धाराः &c.

Why do we say "when denoting a country"? Observe दक्षिपानं the drink of the _Dakshis_.

The words उशीनर and the rest are applied to persons also through the medium of being country-names.
