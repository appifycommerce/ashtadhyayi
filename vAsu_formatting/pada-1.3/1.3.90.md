---
sutra: वाक्यषः
vRtti: क्यषन्ताद्धातोर्वा परस्मैपदं भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The affixes of the _parasmaipada_ are used optionally after the denominative verbs ending in the affix _kyash_.

---
The affix क्यष is ordained by _sutra_ (3.1.12) भृशादिभ्यो भूव्यच् वेर्लोपश्च हलः after the words लोहित &c. These roots take optionally _parasmaipada_. As लोहितायति or ते he reddens. पटपटायति or ते he makes _pat_ _pat_.
