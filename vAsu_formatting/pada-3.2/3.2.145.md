---
sutra: प्रे लपसृद्रुमथवदवसः
vRtti: प्र उपपदे लपादिभ्यो घिनुण्प्रत्ययो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The affix घिनुण् comes after the verbs लप्, सृ, द्रु, मथ, वद् and वस when compounded with the preposition वि.

---
As प्रलापी 'prattling'; प्रसारी 'extending around'; प्रद्रावी 'running away, fugitive'; प्रमाथी 'tormenting'; प्रवादी 'reporting'; प्रवासी 'a traveller'.
 
The root वस् here is the _Bhvadi_ वस् meaning 'to dwell' and is not _Adadi_ meaning 'to cover', because in the latter the _vikarana_ is elided.
