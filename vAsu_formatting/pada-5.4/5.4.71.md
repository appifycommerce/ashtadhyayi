---
sutra: नञस्तत्पुरुषात्
vRtti: परे वक्ष्यमाणा ये राजादयस्तदन्तात्तत्पुरुषात्समासान्तो न भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The _samasanta_ affix does not come after a _Tatpurusha_ compound of words like _rajan_ &c, herein taught, when the negative particle न precedes them.

---
Thus अराजा, असखा, अगौः (5.4.91) and (5.4.92) prohibited). Why do we say "after a _Tatpurusha_ compound?" The same words preceded by the negative particle will take the _samasanta_ affix when forming compounds other than _Tatpurusha_, as अणृचो मानवकः, अधुरं शकटम् ॥ (5.4.74) applied)).
