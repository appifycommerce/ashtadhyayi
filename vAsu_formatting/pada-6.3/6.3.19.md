---
sutra: नेन्सिद्धबध्नातिषु च
vRtti: इन्नन्ते सिद्धशब्दं बध्नातौ च परतः सप्तम्या अलुग्न भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The Locative ending is not preserved before a stem ending in इन्, before the word सिद्ध, and before a word derived from बन्ध् ॥

---
Thus स्थण्डिलशायिन्, सांकाश्यसिद्धः, काम्पिल्यसिद्धः, चक्रबन्धकः, चरकबन्धकः ॥ The compounding takes place by _yoga_-_vibhaga_ of _sutra_ (2.1.40). Some use the word चक्रबन्धः as an illustration under this rule: बन्ध then is derived by अच् of (3.1.134). The बन्ध ending in घञ् is governed by (6.3.13). This _sutra_ is an exception to (6.3.14).
