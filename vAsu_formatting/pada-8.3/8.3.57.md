---
sutra: इण्कोः
vRtti: इण्कोरित्येतदधिकृतं वेदितव्यम् । इत उत्तरं यद्वक्ष्यामः इणः कवर्गाच्चेत्येवं तद्वेदितव्यम् ॥
vRtti_eng: From this, upto the end of the chapter, should be supplied in every _sutra_, the following :- 'when a vowel (with the exception of अ or आ), or a र or a guttural precedes'.

---
The word इण is a _pratyahara_ formed with the second ण् of लण् ॥ It includes all vowels and semivowels excepts अ and आ ॥ Of the semi-vowels र् is only efficient: so that only is taken in the translation. कु means the letters of the क class. Thus इण्कोः is supplied in (8.3.59), to complete the sense. Thus सिषेव, सुष्वाप, अग्निषु, वायुषु, कर्तृषु, हर्तृषु, गीर्षु, धूर्षु, वाक्षु, त्वक्षु ॥

Why do we say "when preceded by इण् or कु"? Observe दास्यति, असौ ॥ Here the affix स of स्यति, and the substitute स in असौ (7.2.106) are not changed to ष ॥
